Regulatory Affairs


Do you plan to install a nuclear or PET cameras in your facility?

Do you need to amend your existing Radioactive Materials License with a new physician, new site or a new radiopharmaceutical?

Do you need help with regulatory issues with Texas Department of State Health Services Radiation Control Program?

We can help! NuTech, Inc offers assistance solving all these issues. We can help you write the new RAM License, we can check on your existing application, we can amend your RAM License according to your requests and current TDSHS regulations. We offer this free service as a courtesy to our existing and potential new customers. If you work within 100 miles of one of our three locations Tyler, College Station, or Wichita Falls give us a call or send us an e-mail. We will save you time of trying to figure out the Texas Administrative Code and the Code of Federal Regulations.
